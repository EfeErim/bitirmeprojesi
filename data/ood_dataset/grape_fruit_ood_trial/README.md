This staged OOD pool is organized for grape fruit adapter notebook trials.

Top-level folders:

- `unsupported_grape_conditions/`: grape images that fall outside the supported fruit disease label set, including unsupported grape conditions and non-target grape symptoms.
- `other_plants_and_fruits/`: other crop leaves, fruits, and plant images used as secondary negatives.
- `easy_negatives/`: clearly irrelevant vineyard-context or non-plant images kept as an easy-negative slice.

Notes:

- The separate `OOD_Üzüm Yaprak/` split from that archive is intentionally not included here.
